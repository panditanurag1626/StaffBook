StaffBook – Job Posting Terms & Conditions

Thank you for choosing StaffBook.

To ensure uninterrupted services, platform integrity, and data security, all employers must strictly adhere to the following Terms & Conditions.

By using StaffBook’s job posting services, resume database access, or recruitment tools, you agree NOT to use the platform in the following manner:

  • Sharing your StaffBook account with others
  • Sending irrelevant or spam communication to candidates
  • Mass downloading resumes
  • Posting jobs for non-recruitment purposes
  • Advertising paid training programs
  • Asking job seekers for money
  • Posting duplicate jobs
  • Redirecting candidates to other job portals
  • Uploading inappropriate or unlawful content

1. Account Sharing

Users must not share their StaffBook login credentials with any individual, organization, or third party.

  • Each account is strictly for authorized internal use.
  • The account holder is responsible for maintaining password confidentiality.
  • Any unauthorized usage must be reported immediately to StaffBook.

Sharing accounts will be treated as a violation and may result in suspension or termination.

2. Sending Irrelevant or Spam Communication

Employers must not send recruitment messages, invitations, or emails to candidates who are not relevant to the job posted.

  • Communication must be strictly recruitment-related.
  • Spam messages or any communication involving monetary requests are prohibited.

Violations may result in account restrictions.

3. Mass Downloading of Resumes

Resume download access is provided strictly for recruitment purposes.

Employers must NOT:

  • Use automated software to scrape or download resumes.
  • Download resumes in bulk for non-recruitment purposes.
  • Share candidate data outside their recruitment team.

Any misuse of candidate information will lead to strict action, including permanent suspension.

4. Job Postings Must Be for Recruitment Only

StaffBook job postings are intended exclusively for hiring purposes.

Job postings must NOT be used for:

  • Data collection
  • Lead generation
  • Marketing
  • Business promotion
  • Surveys
  • Any non-employment-related activity

5. Paid Training Programs Not Allowed

StaffBook is strictly a job and internship posting platform.

Employers must NOT post:

  • Paid training programs
  • Courses requiring upfront fees
  • Certification programs disguised as jobs

6. No Asking Candidates for Money

Employers must not request money from jobseekers for:

  • Interviews
  • Registration
  • Security deposits
  • Training
  • Documentation
  • Equipment
  • Any direct or indirect payment

Any such act will lead to immediate blacklisting from the platform.

7. Duplicate Job Postings

Employers must not post the same job multiple times.

Before posting, employers are expected to verify that the job has not already been listed.

Duplicate postings may be removed without notice.

8. Redirecting Candidates to Other Platforms

StaffBook job postings must not be used to:

  • Redirect candidates to other job portals
  • Ask candidates to register on external websites
  • Promote competing platforms

All recruitment communication must remain within legitimate hiring activity.

9. Inappropriate or Unlawful Content

Users must not upload, post, or share any content that is:

  • Illegal
  • Misleading
  • Harmful
  • Threatening
  • Abusive
  • Defamatory
  • Obscene
  • Discriminatory
  • Offensive on the basis of gender, caste, religion, race, ethnicity, or nationality

StaffBook reserves the right to remove such content immediately.

10. Right to Take Action

StaffBook reserves the right to:

  • Review all job postings
  • Approve, reject, or remove listings
  • Suspend or terminate employer accounts
  • Restrict access without prior notice if policy violations occur

All decisions will be at the sole discretion of StaffBook.

11. Limitation of Responsibility

StaffBook acts as a platform connecting employers and candidates.

StaffBook:

  • Does not guarantee candidate suitability
  • Is not responsible for hiring decisions
  • Is not liable for disputes between employer and candidate

12. Policy Updates

StaffBook may modify these Terms & Conditions at any time.

Continued use of the platform implies acceptance of the updated policy.
